Meet Darlene!

Darlene; the dynamic squirrel who wears many hats as the Chief Executive Mischief Manager of Pawfessional Enterprises. With boundless energy and a knack for organization, Darlene leads her team with wit and charm. From orchestrating acorn heists to coordinating treehouse renovations, there’s never a dull moment with Darlene at the helm. With her bushy tail and sharp mind, she’s the epitome of resourcefulness and agility in the forest.
